Taumutu Natural Hazard Assessment

Making decisions in the face of uncertainty can be hard. In the report created by Mitchell Anderson, we attempt to unpack some of this uncertainty around the potential future at Taumutu. This will allow the decision makers to guide their community into a safe and sustainable future by means of data-driven planning and decreased uncertainty.
